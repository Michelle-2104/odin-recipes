Spicy sparkling cucumber cocktail

DESCRIPTION

This is a spicy yet cool and refreshing cocktail that is perfect for a hot summer

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 thin slices cucumber
  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • 3 dashes hot pepper sauce
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 1 cup club soda, or as needed

DIRECTIONS

  1. Muddle 6 cucumber slices in a cocktail shaker. Add vodka, lime juice, simple syrup and hot sauce. Fill shaker with ice and shake for 30 seconds
  2. Fill two glasses with fresh ice. Strain vodka mixture into the glasses and top off with club soda. Garnish with remaining cucumber slices.
