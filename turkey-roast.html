Marinated Turkey Roast

DESCRIPTION

This is a delicious marinade used for a boneless turkey breast roast.

INGREDIENTS

  • quarter cup olive oil
  • quarter cup soy sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 3 pound boneless turkey breast
  • salt and ground pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Whisk olive oil, soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, and basil together in a bowl and pour into a resealable plastic bag. Add turkey breast, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator, 8 hours to overnight.
