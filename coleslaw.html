DESCRIPTION

Coleslaw without mayonnaise dressing

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 medium head cabbage, shredded
  • 1 carrot
  • quarter onion, chopped
  • quarter cup vegetable oil
  • quarter cup vinegar
  • quarter cup white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a large bowl, combine cabbage, carrot, onion, vegetable oil, vinegar and sugar
  2. Stir until the ingredients are well mixed. Chill in the refrigerator until serving.
