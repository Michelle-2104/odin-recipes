CREAMED-SPINARCH

DESCRIPTION

Creamed spinarch is an American dish which is basically spinarch prepared in a creamy way and can be eaten as a wholesome meal What can be better than a side of creamy, nutmeg-infused spinarch? Perfect to accompany any roast and especially good at Christmas.

INGREDIENTS

  • 25g butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 200ml full-fat milk
  • 400g spinarch
  • 100ml single cream
  • fresh nutmeg, for grating

    • STEPS

    1. Heat 25g butter in a saucepan, then add 1 finely chopped small onion and cook for 5mins until softened
    2. Stir in two tbsp plain flour and cook for 2mins, then slowly start to whisk in 200ml full-fat milk. WHen it has all been incorporated, gently cook for 5mins until the sauce has thickened.
    3. Meanwhile, place 400g spinarch in a large calander. Pour over a kettle full of boiling water until the leaves have wilted (you may have to do this twice)
    4. Place the spinarch in a clean dishcloth, squeeze out any exces liquid, then roughly chop
    5. Stir into the sauce with 100ml single cream, gently heat, then finely grate over some fresh nutmeg and season well
